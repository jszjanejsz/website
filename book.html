Old books exchange

Team : Jane Phoebe Print

2022.11

This is a community project to promote communication among residents in the community as well as to encourage people to meet, communicate and move around more in public spaces. We have set up a used book exchange booth inside the Prince Apartments. Residents can bring their old books that they no longer need to the booth and exchange them for old books brought by other residents. It will create a good flow between books, and we expect that such flow and encounter will invariably bring the residents closer to each other.
