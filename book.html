Takeaway order

Designer : Jane

2022.6

The project was inspired by takeaway during the containment period. It started from the privacy of take-out orders, and then observed the delicate relationship between the constraints we are forced to face and the external supply under the epidemic control. By collecting take-out orders from a few people during the containment period, I documented the entire process with a collage of photographs. With the gradual liberalization of sequestration, the changes in people's external demand also reflect their respective emotions. From homogeneity to diversity, from numbness to habit ......
