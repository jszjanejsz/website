We believe that every girl is unique and beautiful, and that imperfections and differences are what make us who we really are. The girls in the video all have different personalities, from the bold and confident clover girl, to the girl with deep body anxiety, to the girl who is plagued by weight loss, to the girl who is naturally darker than others, to the girl who is not confident because of her birthmark, to the girl who has low self-esteem because of her big mouth and freckles ...... The overall feel of the film is light and cheerful, with images full of movement and bright, jumping colors. The bright colors create a cheerful atmosphere and highlight the different personalities of the girls in the Genesis era.
